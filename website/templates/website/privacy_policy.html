Privacy Policy

How Easy Pass collects, uses, and protects your information

Last updated: January 2026

1. Non-Custodial Notice

Easy Pass is a non-custodial platform. We do not store, control, or have access to your cryptocurrencies, private keys, or wallet credentials. All transactions are executed directly on the blockchain.

2. Information We Collect

  • Email address and basic account details
  • Support communications
  • Device, browser, IP address, and usage analytics
  • Transaction metadata (excluding private keys)

3. Blockchain Data

Cryptocurrency transactions occur on public blockchains. We do not control or alter blockchain records.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Operate and improve the platform
  • Facilitate buy, sell, and swap transactions
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

5. Data Security

We apply reasonable security measures. However, blockchain transactions are irreversible and carry inherent risk.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data where permitted by law.

7. Policy Updates

Continued use of Easy Pass constitutes acceptance of any updates to this policy.
